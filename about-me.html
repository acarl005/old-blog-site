About Me

I was working in a chemistry research lab in LA when I starting futzing around with an open-source program called PyMOL. I tried writing a plug-in for it and got hooked! Having found my true passion, programming, I began Dev Bootcamp to be reborn. I like programming because unlike in chemistry, you can create something out of nothing. When you have an idea, you just have to code it!

I've mastered the art of saying things that aren't technically incorrect, as all scientists must do. I'm really good at cooking a mean breakfast, eating quantities of food that would be fatal to others, and getting a farmer's tan. I enjoy all forms of racquet-based sports. I'll pretty much do anything in exchange for sushi. I can't live without memory foam! Or the internet. I'm an avid fan of music that everybody hates. I enjoy the outdoors. And the indoors. The doorways between the two are just okay. I like to turn innocent games and foul them by turning them into drinking games, such as Mario Kart.
